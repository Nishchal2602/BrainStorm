# Pocket PM OS — Demo Script

## Demo Goal

Demonstrate that Pocket PM is **an AI-native Product Operating System** that transforms PRDs into persistent organizational intelligence instead of one-off AI outputs.

---

# Demo Story

## The Problem (45 seconds)

Today, Product Managers write PRDs in tools like Notion, Confluence, or Google Docs.

They then copy the PRD into ChatGPT or Claude to ask questions like:

* Is this a good PRD?
* Do customers actually have this problem?
* Who are the competitors?
* Should we build this?

The problem is that every conversation is temporary.

The AI forgets previous reviews.

Customer evidence disappears.

Competitive analysis is lost.

Decisions are never connected back to the product.

Teams repeatedly analyze the same documents without building organizational knowledge.

Pocket PM changes that.

Instead of generating temporary conversations, it creates a persistent Product Workspace where every review becomes reusable product intelligence.

Most AI tools answer questions.

Pocket PM builds knowledge.

Customer evidence accumulates.

Competitive intelligence evolves.

Instead of AI replacing PMs, Pocket PM helps product teams continuously build institutional knowledge.
